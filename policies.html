Durham House of Pets Policies:

Financing:

We gladly accept the following forms of payment:

Credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover, as well as PayPal, gift cards, and Apple pay.

Delivery:

We deliver to any customer within 10 miles of our address with a purchase of $25+ with a delivery fee of $8. Any purchase of $100 or more gets delivered for free!

Return Policy:

Our return policy is very easy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it! We accept returns within 50 days of your in-store purchase or from the initial delivery date for delivered purchases.
