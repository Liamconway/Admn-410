About our store:

Store history:

The Durham House of Pets’ journey began in 1968 when founder Kevin Kennedy purchased a property in downtown Durham, New Hampshire. Kennedy, a graduate of the University of New Hampshire always had a love for animals and the town of Durham. The shop started off small, only selling a limited number of products, such as dog, cat, and fish food, dog and cat toys, and dog and cat collars. The business was able to grow under Kennedy’s management, and the shop now has a variety of high-quality items, some of the most popular include dog and cat cages, beds, jackets, and fish tanks. Kennedy retired in 2012 and passed the business on to Omar Johnson, and the shop has become a favorite in the New Hampshire area.
